Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is our website?
A: Our website is an online platform where you can order food from a variety of local restaurants and have it delivered to your door.

Q: How does it work?
A: Simply browse the menu from your favorite restaurant, select the items you want, place your order, and our delivery team will bring it right to you.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Choose a restaurant, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email or notification once your order is placed.

Q: Can I schedule an order for a later time?
A: Yes, you can select a specific time for your order during the checkout process if the restaurant supports scheduled orders.

Q: How long will delivery take?
A: Delivery times vary depending on your location, restaurant preparation time, and traffic conditions. Estimated delivery times are shown before you place your order.

Q: What should I do if my order is late?
A: If your order is late, check the tracking information provided in your order details or contact customer support.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other payment options like Apple Pay or Google Wallet if available.

Q: Are there delivery fees?
A: Yes, a delivery fee may apply and varies by restaurant and distance. The fee will be displayed at checkout.

Q: Can I add a tip for the delivery driver?
A: Yes, you can add a tip during checkout or after your order is delivered.

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Click on "Sign Up" on our homepage, provide your details, and follow the instructions.

Q: How can I view my past orders?
A: Log in to your account, go to "Order History," and you can see all your past orders.

Q: What if I need to change or cancel my order?
A: Once an order is placed, changes or cancellations may not be possible. Contact customer support for assistance as soon as possible.

Q: What if there's an issue with my food?
A: Please contact customer support immediately. We will work with the restaurant to address any concerns with your order.

Q: Are there vegetarian/vegan/gluten-free options?
A: Yes, many of our partnered restaurants offer special dietary options. You can use filters to find items that suit your dietary needs.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us via email at [your-email@example.com], phone at [your phone number], or by using the live chat feature on our website.
